Join us for a spectacular time at Butterfield Park in Castle Rock, Colorado, where you can
walk or run 3.1 miles at the first ever Puppy Power 5k!
Register now!
Adults $25 - Kids $15
Why?
We are trying to spread the word about good organizations (such as the Humane Society of the United States and the Denver Dumb Friends League) and bad happenings (such as puppy mills).
What?
You'll get to enjoy an awesome walk / run, of course, but you will get more than just a little exercise. When you join us you will:
  • have lots of fun! From the run itself to standing around, drinking lemonade and talking to friends, it is sure to be a big hit!
  • be raising money and awareness to stop cruel puppy mills. Part of your entry fee will go to the Humane Society, part to the Dumb Friends League, and part to make this event happen.
  • get active with your kids, pets, husband, wife, family, friends, or whoever you invite along!
Who?
We are a group of 5th graders determined to do what we can to help animals in need. This fun run seemed like a great way to raise awareness, raise funds for our favorite cause, and have a great time!
